Simon Riley

Ghost is a popular character in the Call of Duty (COD) series, known for his iconic skull-patterned mask and stealthy demeanor. His full name is Simon Riley, and he first appeared in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (2009). Ghost is a highly skilled and fearless special forces operator, often involved in covert missions. His mysterious background, masked identity, and calm, deadly precision have made him a fan-favorite among COD players. He was reintroduced in the Modern Warfare (2019) reboot and continues to be a central figure in the franchise.
